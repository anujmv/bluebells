About Us

Who We Are

Comfort, convenience and affordability at its best

Blue Bells is a perfect blend of luxury and comfort. While our great location makes it easy to commute and travel, we offer you a nurturing environment to grow in, with all top-notch facilities and services. From spacious apartments to contemporary amenities, we make your life simpler than you could ever imagine!

What We Offer

Experience the Comfortable Living

We, at Blue Bells believe in creating a perfect living experience for our residents. That's why we offer everything that you need to feel at home: modern appliances, ample storage space, and good-looking interiors – all in a prime location. Come home to the lifestyle of your choice!

Hygienic Interiors

Enjoy clean and hygienic spaces with strict COVID-19 protocols in place.

Healthy, Nutritious Meals

Delicious, healthy and home-made meals thrice a day for a healthy living.

Round The Clock Security

Dedicated wardens and property incharges to look after the overall security.

24*7 Medical Assistance

Quick, immediate medical assistance is just a phone call away in case of any emergency.
